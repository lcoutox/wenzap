# PRODUCT_VISION.md

## Product name

Nexbrain

## One-line description

Nexbrain is a platform for creating AI agents connected to a company’s data, channels and business processes.

## Vision

Nexbrain helps companies apply artificial intelligence to real business operations.

The platform allows a company to create AI agents, connect them to knowledge bases, publish them across communication channels, manage conversations through an inbox, organize work in pipelines and integrate agents with external tools.

The long-term vision is to become an AI operations layer for businesses.

## Core idea

Nexbrain combines:

* AI agents
* knowledge bases
* communication channels
* inbox
* pipelines
* actions
* automations
* integrations
* analytics
* human control

The product should not be limited to answering questions.

The product should help companies operate workflows with AI.

## Positioning

Nexbrain is not only a chatbot builder.

Nexbrain is an AI agent orchestration platform.

A chatbot mainly answers messages.

A Nexbrain agent should be able to:

* answer questions
* qualify leads
* collect information
* summarize conversations
* suggest responses
* move items in pipelines
* call webhooks
* trigger automations
* request human assistance
* interact with business tools

## Target customers

Nexbrain is initially designed for small and medium-sized businesses that want to use AI but do not have technical teams to build custom AI systems.

Potential customer profiles:

* companies with repetitive customer support
* companies that receive leads through website or messaging channels
* companies with sales teams using WhatsApp or chat
* companies with internal documents and FAQs
* companies that need simple AI automation
* companies that want AI but still need human control

Possible verticals:

* automotive dealerships
* real estate agencies
* clinics
* schools and courses
* e-commerce stores
* service providers
* agencies
* B2B sales teams
* local businesses

## Main product promise

> Create AI agents for support, sales and operations, connected to your company’s knowledge, channels and workflows.

## Strategic difference

Traditional chatbot platforms focus on conversations.

Nexbrain should focus on business operations.

The product should connect conversation intelligence with operational execution.

This means that a conversation should not be isolated. It can generate:

* a contact
* a lead
* a pipeline card
* a task
* a summary
* a tag
* a score
* a follow-up
* a webhook event
* a human handoff

## Product principles

### 1. Simple before powerful

The product should be easy to understand before it becomes feature-rich.

### 2. Operational before generic

Nexbrain should solve concrete business workflows, not only provide generic AI chat.

### 3. Human control by default

The user must be able to pause, assume, review or override AI behavior.

### 4. Multi-tenant from day one

The architecture must support multiple customer organizations safely from the start.

### 5. Modular integrations

External tools should be connected through a modular integration system.

### 6. Knowledge-driven agents

Agents should be grounded in company knowledge whenever possible.

### 7. Traceable AI

Important AI actions and responses should be logged for debugging, quality and trust.

### 8. Expandable MVP

The MVP can be small, but the architecture should allow future expansion into integrations, automations and marketplace-like modules.

## What Nexbrain is not

Nexbrain is not initially:

* a full CRM replacement
* a full workflow automation platform like Zapier/Make
* a model training platform
* a custom LLM provider
* a generic internal chat tool only
* a heavy enterprise platform at launch
* a no-code builder with unlimited complexity

## Long-term opportunity

Nexbrain can evolve into a business AI operations platform where companies install and configure specialized agents for different departments and workflows.

Examples:

* Sales agent
* Support agent
* SDR agent
* Collections agent
* Onboarding agent
* HR agent
* E-commerce agent
* Real estate agent
* Automotive agent
* Internal knowledge agent

Each agent can connect to specific knowledge, channels, tools and pipelines.

## Core thesis

Companies do not only need AI that talks.

They need AI that understands business context, interacts with tools and helps operate workflows.

Nexbrain should be built around this thesis.
